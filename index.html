Smart Solver 3D color input (3×3) + Pyraminx

Smart Solver lets users solve their real cubes: for 3×3 using 3D color input, and for Pyraminx using scramble-based solving. 2×2 and 4×4 are shown as visual models here and fully supported in Gameplay & Practice.

Pyraminx Smart Solver: enter the scramble you did on your real pyramid and get reverse moves to solve it.
Drag to rotate the cube in 3D. Click a color below, then click stickers to paint them.
Smart Solver painting + solving is enabled for 3×3. Pyraminx Smart Solver uses scrambles. 2×2 and 4×4 are visual only here; use Gameplay & Practice for their interaction.
1) Hold your Pyraminx solved, then apply these moves on your real cube to scramble it.
2) Paste the same scramble here and click “Solve Pyraminx from Scramble”.
3) Follow the reverse moves shown to solve your real Pyraminx.
For 3×3: each color (U,R,F,D,L,B) can be used at most 9 times total (1 center + 8 stickers).
Carefully match your real cube’s scrambled state, then press “Solve (3×3)”.

Project Summary

This single page demonstrates your final year project: a Rubik's Cube solver and interactive learning app.

  • Smart 3×3 solver with 3D color-based input for real cubes.
  • Pyraminx Smart Solver from user-entered scrambles.
  • Multi-cube practice: 2×2, 3×3, 4×4, Pyraminx.
  • Live twisty-player cube with 360° mouse rotation, move buttons and animations.
  • Random scrambles, reset, hints, timing with best-score tracking, and 4×4 wide moves.
  • Purchase links to integrate basic e-commerce.

Future scope: Smart Solver support for 2×2 / 4×4 via color input, camera-based scanning, AR overlays, user profiles, and leaderboards.
